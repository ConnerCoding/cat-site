What Am I?

I am a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. The only domesticated species in the family Felidae and often referred to as the domestic cat to distinguish it from the wild (not-as-cool) members of the family. I am specifically of the house cat variety, as opposed to a farm cat or feral cat (gross!). I am valued by my humans for companionship and my ability to hunt vicious rodents (they call them "toys"?). In other words, I am awesome.
